Why Choose Copper?

Copper Gutter Benefits

  • Durability: Copper gutters are renowned for their exceptional durability. Copper is a naturally weather-resistant material that can withstand harsh elements, such as heavy rain, snow, and extreme temperatures, without rusting or deteriorating. This durability ensures that your gutters will have a long lifespan, providing reliable protection for your home against water damage.
  • Aesthetics: Copper gutters offer timeless beauty and elegance to any property. As they age, copper develops a distinctive patina that adds character and charm to your home or building. The rich, warm tones of copper complement a variety of architectural styles, making it a versatile choice that enhances the overall curb appeal of your property.
  • Low Maintenance: One of the key benefits of copper gutters is their low maintenance requirements. Unlike other gutter materials, copper doesn't require regular painting or coating to maintain its appearance. The natural patina that forms over time protects the copper from corrosion, reducing the need for frequent upkeep. Occasional cleaning to remove debris is typically all that's needed to keep your copper gutters functioning optimally.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Copper is a highly sustainable material, making copper gutters an eco-friendly choice. Copper is a naturally occurring resource that can be recycled without any loss of its inherent properties. By choosing copper gutters, you're contributing to reducing waste and promoting a more sustainable environment. Additionally, copper gutters have a long lifespan, reducing the need for frequent replacements, further minimizing environmental impact.

HOW WE WORK

Top Working Process

It's worth noting that the installation process may vary depending on the specific requirements of each property. Working with a professional copper gutter installer ensures that the entire process is carried out with precision and expertise, resulting in a durable and aesthetically pleasing copper gutter system that effectively protects your property from water damage.

1

Assessment and Planning

The installation process begins with a thorough assessment of the property. A professional gutter installer will evaluate the roofline, architectural features, and drainage requirements to determine the optimal placement and design of the copper gutter system. This step involves measuring the dimensions, identifying potential challenges, and planning for downspout placement to ensure effective water flow.

2

Custom Fabrication

Copper gutters are typically custom fabricated to fit the specific dimensions and architectural style of the property. Skilled craftsmen will shape and solder the copper sheets to create seamless gutters that align perfectly with the roofline. This precise customization ensures a secure fit and a visually appealing finished product that complements the property's aesthetics.

3

Installation

Once the custom copper gutters are fabricated, the installation process begins. The existing gutters, if any, may need to be removed and the fascia inspected for any necessary repairs. The copper gutters are then carefully positioned and securely attached to the fascia or roof edge using appropriate hangers or brackets. Proper slope and alignment are crucial to ensure efficient water flow towards the downspouts.
